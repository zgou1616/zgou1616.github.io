About

I am an Applied Scientist interested in machine learning, foundation models, agentic AI systems, and scientific machine learning. My work focuses on building reliable AI systems and applying modern AI methods to engineering and scientific problems.

I received my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Southern California. My research spans uncertainty quantification, stochastic dynamics, surrogate modeling, diffusion models, and combustion.

Current interests: AI agents, trustworthy LLM systems, multimodal reasoning, diffusion models, and AI for physical sciences.
